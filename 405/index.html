Status Update

July 18, 2013

Ian and Matt made it to Budapest without incident today. We leave for Bucharest on Saturday morning. We’re on our way out now to explore the city. We tracked down one other team spending the night in Budapest, so we’re meeting them for a drink. It’s nice to find other stragglers — most teams are at least in Romania by now. We’re happy to drag our feet in Europe as long as we can.

Nelson at the helm in the Hungarian countryside. Beard+wind=peace.
