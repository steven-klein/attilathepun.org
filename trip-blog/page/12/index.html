Thank you, Sonia and Brian!

July 27, 2013

As previously reported, we finally have our official vehicle registration, no thanks to the royal baby. But endless thanks to two gracious friends who executed heroic feats in London on Attila The Pun’s behalf. Thank you Sonia Crapanzano and Brian Scully who, against all odds, managed to recover our registration from the Royal Mail and forward it without delay to Bucharest. We’re not sure where we’d be without you. Actually, we know exactly where we’d be. In Romania. Making no progress toward Mongolia.
